Why Join?

Electrium Mobility will let you apply content from your major that you're supposed to be learning. We believe in taking a hands on approach to learning. You can make friends and meet a team of really cool people!

Impact

Green vehicles are good for the environment, you can make an impact by choosing clean transportation

Learning

We build entire projects from scratch so you can watch and contribute to the entire process of building an electric vehicle

Networking

You can meet other people who share the same passions, interests, and values as you do

How to Apply?

1

Submit your Application

Write your application and submit it to our application form once we release applications!

2

Interview with us

If we like your written application, you'll be invited to an interview! Don't worry, it's fairly casual!

3

Wait for decision

After your interview, all that's left is the waiting game! Expect a response from us within three weeks of your interview
