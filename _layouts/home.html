Our Mission

Electrium Mobility aims to teach how to create electric vehicles in a beginner friendly way.

Portability

Our vehicles are by design, incredibly easy to carry around

Sustainability

We care about the environment so our vehicles use electricity

Affordability

Our vehicles are fairly low cost to make

Who we are

We are a group of students united by a common goal of creating better modes of transportation for the world!

What we do

We create electric vehicles such as bikes, skateboards, scooters, etc. from scratch, allowing us to walk students through the entire design process
